About this Item

First edition first printing. Hardback book is tight clean and square. Boards sharp and clean, title gilt perfect. Text block clean, lovely. Feels unread. Fine condition. Jacket likewise fine, only very light handling marks to the gloss. Signed limited edition. For grading accuracy books are photographed without a protective book cover. This book is fitted with a non adhesive archival quality book protector to make a worthy addition to your collection. Note we do not use stock images, the book pictured is the book described. A fine copy of this scarce collectible first edition first printing title, signed to the limitation page by Joel Lane the author and Conrad Williams, who wrote the introduction. Limited edition this copy being unnumbered of 100 copies that were signed by both. There was a further 200 copies signed by Joel Lane only. An unsettling novel, a man discovers disturbing video tapes in a house he bought which leads him on a journey in to gritty darkness, from the writer who died too young and winner of World Fantasy Award for this collection in 2013 and the British Fantasy Award twice. A very difficult book to obtain. Seller Inventory # 006033
