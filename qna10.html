How to update your credit card details

Sep 6, 2022

Sign in to your seller or buyer account to update your credit card details.

How to add a new credit card

  • Go to My Account and click Manage my Credit Cards in the My Personal Info section.
  • Select the type of credit card you want to add from the Select a Credit Card dropdown menu.
  • Enter your credit card number, expiration date and cardholder name.
  • If your billing address and shipping address are the same, you can import your shipping address from the Address Book by selecting the right address from the Billing Address dropdown menu.
  • If your billing address and shipping address are different, select New Address from the Billing Address dropdown menu.
  • Click Save Changes.

Your credit card data is encrypted on our secure server and is not shared with anyone. For security reasons, a credit card number cannot be changed after it has been saved. Only the expiration date and address of an existing card can be updated.

How to update the expiry date of an existing credit card

  • Go to My Account and click Manage my Credit Cards in the My Personal Info section.
  • To update the expiration date of an existing credit card, select your credit card from the dropdown menu, update the month and year fields, and click Save Changes.
  • To delete the credit card, click Delete this card. A pop-up window will prompt you to confirm that you want to delete the credit card as a payment method. Click Confirm Delete.
