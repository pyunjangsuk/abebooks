How to create an account

Jan 24, 2024

You can create your account before searching for items or during checkout. If you've already added items to your shopping basket, you will be prompted to create an account when you're ready to checkout.

Follow these steps to set up your buyer account:

  • Go to Create Account and enter your name and email address.
  • Enter a secure password.
  • Click Create your account.
  • Verify your email address by entering the One Time Password (OTP) sent to your email.

You can also sign in to our sites using your Amazon account credentials by following these steps:

  • Go to Create Account.
  • Select Login with Amazon.
  • Enter the Amazon email address and password on the Amazon authentication page and select Sign in.
  • Select Allow.
  • Complete the setup by creating an AbeBooks account with your Amazon credentials, verify your email address and select Create your AbeBooks account.

If you already have an account with us, you can connect it to your Amazon account by following these steps:

  • Go to the sign in page.
  • Select Login with Amazon.
  • Enter the Amazon email address and password on the Amazon authentication page and select Sign in.
  • Select Allow.
  • In the finish registration screen, select the Connect it to your Amazon account link.
  • Enter the email address associated with your AbeBooks account again and select sign in.

How to choose a secure password

A strong password is longer than 8 characters, contains random capitalization, numbers and special characters, and is not easy to guess. Using dictionary words, your name, email address or other easily identifiable information in the password is not recommended.

Manage Your Account

  • To set your preferred currency, go to My Account Information.
  • To add, edit and delete shipping addresses, go to My Address Book.
  • To add, edit and delete your credit card details, go to Manage My Credit Cards.
