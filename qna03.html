How to cancel your order

Feb 1, 2023

You can cancel an order as long as it has not yet been processed by the seller.

How to cancel your order

  • Go to My Account and click My Purchases.
  • Find the item you want to cancel and click Cancel this Item. (This link only appears if the seller has not yet processed the order.)
  • A new window will open with a list of items in the order. Choose the items in the order you want to cancel and click Cancel selected items in this order. You will receive an email confirming the cancellation.

We'll send you an email to confirm the cancellation.

If the seller has processed your order, the item may have already been shipped. If this is the case, you'll need to request a return instead and ship the item back to the seller after you receive it.

You can also contact the seller directly to confirm whether the order has been shipped. If it has not, you can request that the seller cancel the order before shipment.
