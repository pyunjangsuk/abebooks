How to send your item to a different address

Sep 6, 2022

You can Send items to different addresses on our sites, you just need to provide the address you want the item shipped to when you place your order.

How to Provide the Destination Address:

  • After you've added the item to your basket, click the Proceed to Checkout button.
  • Select a payment method and enter your payment details and billing address in the spaces provided.
  • Click Save & Continue.
  • Optional - save the payment method and shipping address for future purchases, so you can check out more quickly next time.
  • On the Order Summary page, click the Edit button next to the Shipping Address.
  • Scroll down to the bottom of the page and enter the address you'd like the book shipped to. Click the Save Changes button.
  • Confirm that the address was entered correctly and then select Use this Address.
  • Select a shipping speed from the dropdown menu.
  • Confirm your payment method, shipping address, books ordered, and shipping speed, and then click Place Order Now.

On the Thank You page, your Sales Order Number will be displayed in red. Make note of the number for future reference. Your order details, including the Sales Order Number, will be included in the Order Confirmation Email.

If the book is a gift, you may wish to send a note to the seller. Click My Account, then My Purchases, and find the order in the list and click Contact Seller.
