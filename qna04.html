How to change your account information

Jan 24, 2024

Sign in to your buyer account to update your account information.

How to change your email address

  • Go to My Account and click My name, email and password in the My Personal Info section.
  • Edit the field(s) you want to update and click Save Changes.
  • If you change your email address, a One Time Password (OTP) will be sent to your new address. Verify your email address by entering the OTP sent to your email. Once you've successfully changed your email address, you will need to re-enter your password to sign into your account.

If you want to sign in using your Amazon credentials, you will need to sign out of your account and sign back in using the Login with Amazon feature.

How to change your shipping address

  • Go to My Account and click My Address Book.
  • To add a new shipping address, complete the Add/Edit Address form and click Save Changes.
  • To edit or delete previously saved shipping addresses, click either the Edit or Delete option below the address you want to change.
  • To edit an existing address, click the Edit option below that address, make your changes, and click Save Changes.
  • To delete an existing address, click the Delete option below that address. Clicking Delete will immediately delete the address.

How to change your credit card and billing address

  • Go to My Account and click Manage my Credit Cards.
  • Update your credit card information and billing address and click Save Changes.

How to change your preferred currency and nickname

  • Go to My Account and click Account Preferences.
  • Select your preferred currency, enter your nickname and click Save Changes.
