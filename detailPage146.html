About this Item

New. See scans and description. New York: Hachette Filippachi Magazines, Inc., 1998. Volume X, Number 1 of Woman's Day Super Specials, that being Weekend Decorating Projects, 1998. Edited by Carolyn M. Gatto. Quarto, illustrated perfect-bound wraps, a hearty 136 pp. New; demure as-produced wrinkle at corner of contents pages (zoom on scan), otherwise flawless, stem-to-stern. All 136 pages are chock-a-block with decorating ideas in full color photography as well as the how-to nuts and bolts. See contents page scans, but a few items are: Welcome Autumn; Breakfast in Bed; Country Charm; Earthy Materials; Fruit Themes; Finger Painting; Sticks & Stones; Color Play; City Elegance; Designed by Nature. More. A serious genre collectible from 1998. Ships in a new, sturdy, protective box - not a bag. Now very scarce. PR32. Seller Inventory # 62444
