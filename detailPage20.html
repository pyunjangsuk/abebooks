8vo. SIGNED and dated Nov. 3, 1993 by Anne Rice to title page. Small damp stain to front board, minor chafing to spine foot. Dust jacket is price clipped, toned to flaps. About fine. A contemporary Lolita, Belinda is the sensual story of a postmodern nymphet and her provocative relationship with her considerably older lover.
