How to close your account

Sep 6, 2022

We would prefer that you remain a customer with us and we are willing to help with any problems you're having. If you would like to speak to a representative before deciding to close your account, please Contact Us and we will respond within 1 business day.

Some of the most common reasons for wanting to close an account are simple to resolve. We can help you:

  • Unsubscribe from our email list.
  • Update your email address or personal information.
  • Address concerns about the security of personal information.
  • Solve problems with a specific order or seller.

If you still wish to close your account, please submit your request through our Online Form.

Once your account is closed, all your account details will be permanently deleted, including your order history, Wants, and any saved credit card details. Once deleted, your account cannot be restored. You will need to create a new account if you wish to order from us again.
