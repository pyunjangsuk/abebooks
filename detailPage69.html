About this Item

Small octavo, pictorial wrappers. First edition. The first publication of these plays in any language. In addition to the plays there is an interview with Ustinov, first published in SOVIET THEATER, March 1988, and a 3-page essay, "The Plays of Lev Ustinov" by Joseph Robinette. In the 1960s and 1970s Lev Ustinov (1923-2009) was the most popular children's dramatist in Russia and by 1990 his plays had been performed more than 25,000 times by over 500 theaters in eighty-eight countries. Small trade paperback format. Covers lightly foxed, a very good or better, unread copy. (#167988). Seller Inventory # 167988
