Estimated delivery date explained

Sep 6, 2022

When you submit an order through our sites, we forward it to the bookseller from whom you ordered. When that bookseller processes the order, we send you an Order Processed email.

Important Order Processed Email Information:

  • Date Processed: mm/dd/yyyy
  • Approximate Shipping Speed: x – x business days
  • Orders are usually shipped within 2 business days

Sellers will usually put your order in the mail within 2 business days, not that you should expect to receive your book within 2 business days.

You can view full details of your order through your buyer account. Click My Purchases in the Members Menu. If you would like additional information on your shipment, you can use the Contact Seller link on the My Purchases page to send a message directly to the Seller.

Your order may arrive before the Estimated Delivery Date – this date is the last date you may expect it based on the shipping speed you selected.

We calculate the estimated delivery date based on business days (Monday to Friday, excluding weekends). Please note that holidays or customs processing may affect delivery time.
