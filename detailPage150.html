About this Item

A very strong Very Good, approaching Near Fine. See scans and description. Devon, PA: Lapidary Journal / Primedia Enthusiast Group, 2002. The February, 2002 issue of the venerable Lapidary Journal, that being "The Step by Step" issue. Quarto, illustrated perfect-bound wraps, 98 pp. Handsome condition - technically Very Good, due to a few too many small peripheral touches at the covers and a very faint musty scent, already dissipating, likely to be gone by the time of purchase. The always expert text throughout is complimented with the best of color photography in the genre.
